Brawl Stars GC

The Brawl Stars GC is a global digital collective composing of seven members (as of 2025) United primarily by chaos, questionable humor, and a collective inability to leave. founded in 2024 by Lilith Mateo Alfaro (L), with the idea co-developed by Lucky Star. Initially conceived as a private space to “bully each other without censors on Brawl Stars,” the group has since evolved into a sociological experiment involving chaos, emotional damage, and the occasional display of friendship, operating within multiple time zones and religions. It has became a socio-cultural phenomenon known for emotional instability, and general disregard for peace and quiet.
